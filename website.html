Where To Find Me?


This July, I started working as an Associate Engineer at Merck, one of the largest pharmaceutical companies in the world. I am in the Technical Operations department ensuring that the manufacture of vaccines is both safe and at the utmost quality.

I'm living in South Philly right now and I grew up in Central Jersey so I will go home pretty often because of how close it is. Feel free to reach out to me if you're ever in New York City, Philly, or even DC!
